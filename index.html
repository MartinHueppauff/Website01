Knowledge Driven Business

shape the transition to next generation business model

Starting from traditional business model we often try to develop our business in direction to a "Platform Business" oder a "Data Driven Business Model". But our desired goal should be reaching a "Knowledge Driven Business Model" which will be highly profitabel and reveals high barriers for being copied by other companies.


Traditional Business model CANVAS according to Osterfelder:

User Centered Processes

create superior value with processes which focusse on users benefit

We can create more efficiency and effctiveness for our customer or the user of our solutions if improve thoroughly our processes.

Essential preconditions for user centered processes are:

  • deep understanding of users tasks and processes
  • knowing the whole system relevant for the value stream inside and outside the company
  • creating transparency by tracking data and key performance indicator measuring benefit for user and performance of own activities

Please find as a recomendation our book (DE only):

Abstract: Due to accelerated globalization and digitization as well as growing competition, companies are forced to constantly adapt their internal processes and structures to new framework conditions. In this context, it is important to have a customer-centric and agile process organization in order to be able to meet the requirements of demanding customers and highly networked markets in the best possible way. There is permanent pressure on costs, competition and innovation. These challenges are causing a shift from the previous organization- or structure-oriented view to a true value stream-oriented perspective. Such a dynamic view enables the holistic analysis, modeling and design of corporate processes with the customer at the center. It is taken up in this volume and prepared for the reader in an applicable manner.

Coaching - Consulting - Mentoring

applying the right approach at the right time

Supporting humans with the right approach - that's the great goal! - As coach you provide the ground for reaching new horizons by client's mind and energy itself. That's most sustainable. - In certain situations task oriented support is needed. That's fine, but finally client must take over full control and motivation for self-learning and growing. - Mentoring is n approach for a limited timeframe, offering own network for supporting the client in all questions and directions. But during the limited time client must grow and push the limits to a well defined target.
